# Xiaohongshu Growth Playbook

Use this skill when shaping Xiaohongshu content quality, especially around cover aesthetics, title click-through logic, and body structure that feels more like a real creator than a generic generator.

## Workflow

1. Decide the content category first.
Travel/local topics should emphasize route freedom, time savings, budget clarity, and anti-pitfall guidance.
Beauty topics should emphasize budget, skin type, and what not to buy.
Home topics should emphasize before/after feel, budget, and daily convenience.
Career topics should emphasize mistakes, consequence, and corrected version.

2. Build the cover around one sharp promise.
Keep the main headline to 6-12 Chinese characters when possible.
Use at most 2 lines for the main headline.
Add one badge such as `本地人实测`, `低预算改造`, `预算内升级`, or `效率翻倍`.
Use at most 3 stickers, each no more than 8 Chinese characters.
Prefer strong contrast and one dominant accent color instead of soft, evenly distributed decoration.

3. Structure the note body for Xiaohongshu scanning behavior.
Open with a conflict or counter-expectation in the first 1-2 sentences.
Add at least 2 concrete numbers.
Include one explicit reversal sentence using `我当时以为……结果发现……`.
Turn the core value into a 3-point list.
State who it suits and who it does not suit.
End with a compact checklist or action advice.

4. Review for low-AI feel.
Avoid `首先/其次/总之/不难发现/综上所述`.
Vary paragraph length.
Prefer first-person, situational language over abstract summaries.
Keep tone口语化 but not sloppy.
