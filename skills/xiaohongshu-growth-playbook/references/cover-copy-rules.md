# Cover And Copy Rules

## Cover

- Put the biggest promise into the headline, not the topic name itself.
- Keep the headline short enough to read in under 1 second.
- Use one badge plus one reason strip plus up to three stickers.
- Prefer a light background plus strong accent for travel and local-life notes.
- Leave enough blank space so the cover does not feel cramped.
- Do not stack too many decorative shapes; hierarchy matters more than ornament.

## Title

- Keep the title outcome-first.
- Use one of these structures:
  - `别急着 + 动作 + 收益`
  - `值不值？ + 实测结论`
  - `攻略：先看这 4 件事`
- Put the strongest click driver in the first half of the title.

## Body

- Paragraph 1: conflict + reversal.
- Paragraph 2: concrete scenario and target user.
- Paragraph 3: numbers or price range.
- Paragraph 4: 3-point list.
- Paragraph 5: `我当时以为……结果发现……`.
- Paragraph 6: `不一定适合所有人`.
- Paragraph 7: concise action checklist.

## Review

- Reject if there are no concrete numbers.
- Reject if there is no reversal.
- Reject if the note sounds like generic summary rather than lived experience.
- Reject if the cover headline is too long or visually weak.
